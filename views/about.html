About ShareCase

Empowering students to showcase, collaborate, and innovate.

ShareCase is a platform built for university students to share academic projects, connect with peers, and create professional portfolios. Designed by a mechanical engineering student, ShareCase makes past projects accessible for inspiration, driving innovation. From robotics designs to software prototypes, this is your space to stand out.

Our Mission

We aim to foster a collaborative space where students can learn from each other, minimize redundant work, and spark fresh ideas. ShareCase’s project repository lets you build on existing knowledge while pushing for originality.

Protecting Your Work

Your project’s integrity and ownership matter to us. Here’s how we keep your work safe:

  • Learn, Don’t Copy: ShareCase is for inspiration, not resubmission. Projects are shared to guide and inspire, with ethical use in mind.
  • Academic Integrity: Your professors set the rules for academic honesty. Always check course guidelines before using project references.
  • Full Ownership: You retain complete ownership of your uploaded projects. By sharing, you confirm you’re the creator and agree to educational use.
  • Privacy Options: Control who sees your work by adjusting visibility settings (public or private) in Settings.

User Help & Guide

Maximize your ShareCase experience with these tips:

  • Build Your Profile: Add your major, department, and LinkedIn in Settings to boost visibility and credibility.
  • Search Smart: Use the search bar for projects or users by keywords, or apply filters (course, year, type) for better results.
  • Team Up: Include collaborators when uploading projects to share credit. Search by name or email to add them.
  • Manage Your Work: Edit or view published and draft projects on your Profile. Save drafts for works in progress.
  • Earn Points: Gain points through uploads, likes, or comments. Check your points in the header dropdown.
  • Get Support: Reach out to support@sharecase.com for technical help or feedback.

Frequently Asked Questions

From your Profile, click “Upload Project” to add a project. Include files, a description, tags, and choose to publish or save as a draft.

Published projects are visible to all ShareCase users by default. Set your profile to private in Settings to limit access.
